Workshops

We believe workshops can be incredibly helpful for families who are new to America. Road to Healthcare offers various workshops with small admission fees.

The Basics of American Healthcare

This workshop consists of two in-person 90 minute classes. By listening and directly interacting with certified speakers, you'll learn :

  • Costs associated with Healthcare
  • Different ways consumers get coverage
  • Purposes and types of healve insurace
  • Immigrant friendly healthcare services
  • Lower cost alternatives

Overcoming Healthcare as an Immigrant

This workshop consists of one in-person 60 minute class focusing on overcoming problems Immigrants may have accessing Healthcare. Here's what this workshop offers :

  • A safe space to address issues specific to you and recieve feedback on the best course of action.
  • Emotional support groups.
  • Advice on what do to when Healthcare isn't available.
  • Direct networking with licensed doctors focused on bringing Healthcare to your community.

Foundations of financial wellness

This workshop consits of one in-person 60 minute class. Using group discussions, learning circles, and online rescources, you'll learn to:

  • Budget to create savings.
  • Strategically reduce debt.
  • Understand credit.
