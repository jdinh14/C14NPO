"Our mission is to raise awareness and address the lack of adequate access
to public health assistance and insurance for undocumented individuals
in the United States of America.

With the unprecedented international migration seen in recent years,
policies that limit health care access have become prevalent.
Barriers to health care for undocumented immigrants go beyond policy
and range from financial limitations, to discrimination and fear of deportation.

We acknowledge the limitations civilians may face when seeking assistance, but there are options.
To further assist the public, we focus on providing professional recommendations
of systems that may offer a low-cost alternative to full access medical needs.

We are dedicated to empowering and supporting our community by providing
essential resources and advocating for reform to ensure equitable medical access for all."
